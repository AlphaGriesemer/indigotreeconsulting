INDIGO TREE CONSULTING IS AN INDIANAPOLIS BASED FIRM SPECIALIZING IN TREE RELATED CONSULTING SERVICES.

Indigo Tree Consulting, LLC was founded by Andrew Mertz in 2016. Andrew is a Registered Consulting Arborist and Board Certified Master Arborist with over 18 years of professional arboricultural and restoration ecology experience. He is also a graduate of the School of Public and Environmental Affairs at Indiana University with a Masters of Public Affairs. Professional experience includes over a decade of public service with the City of Indianapolis as a restoration ecologist and arborist for Indy Parks and Recreation. He served as Urban Forestry Manager for the City of Indianapolis from 2010 to 2012. Andrew was a consulting arborist for a locally owned tree care company from 2012 to 2016. As a consulting arborist, he prepared tree and landscape appraisals, tree risk assessments, municipal tree inventories, and specifications for tree care services including tree removal, tree pruning, and plant health care.
